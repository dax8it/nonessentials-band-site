## About

We are not just another cover band. Our repertoire is a carefully curated mix of classic rock and alternative rock hits that actually work in a room full of people who came to have a good time.

From 70s staples to 80s guitars, 90s grit, and early-2000s singalongs, we keep the set moving and the energy up without relying on costumes, gimmicks, or fake nostalgia. The point is simple: play the songs people love, and play them like they matter.

Based in Northern New Jersey and working across the tristate area, The Non-Essentials are available for bookings and built for lively crowds, big choruses, and late nights.
